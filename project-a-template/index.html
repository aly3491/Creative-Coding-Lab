Maki Maki

Scientific Name:

Splendens Tsubasas

Discovered by / at:

Annie Yang, 2022

Family:

Splendens

Habitat:

They live in the deep part of the ocean where only little sunlight is presence. Around them are seagrasses and have little white berries in it that they feed on when they're hungry. They like to be alone so usually they settle where there are no other animals around them.

Appearance:

Purple body with blue wings and eyes.

Key Characteristics:

  • Hates being around things, esspecially people
  • Has valuable wings that humans want to take
  • Eats little white berries

What we know so far about Maki? Maki likes to swim around in his free time because that's where he feels the most at ease and happy. In his free time, he likes to munch on the little white berries on the sea grasses around him. Unfortantly Maki has poor vision due to being in the dark all the time with little sunlight exposed to him so he can only see things if it's very close to him. Whenever he feels human coming in front of him, he will swim away from them.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature It's best for you to not interact with Maki. As said before, these creatures are really afraid of human. And this is due to them having these valuable wings on their backs that are worth a lot of money. At the very beginning of discovering them in 2022, they have experience trauamtic events of seeing their friends and families die due to humans capturing them and taking the wings away. Over the years, they've become more isolated from each other and afriad of other beings.
