☁️ Makiatos- Maki 🐟✨

Scientific Name:

Splendens Tsubasas

Discovered by / at:

Annie Yang when she went diving, 2022

Family:

Splendens

Habitat:

These creatures live in the deep ends of the ocean alone because they prefer to be all by themselves. On top of their habitats are humans who are trying to fish them out all the time and occasionally white food participles float down for them to feed on.

Appearance:

Purple body with blue wings, eyes, and nose.

Key Characteristics:

  • Hates being around things
  • Fearful of humans
  • Has valuable wings that humans kill them for

What we know so far about Maki? Maki is one of the very few Makiatos out there in the world right now. He likes to swim around in his free time and enjoys eating the little white food particles that float down from the top of the ocean. Maki doesn’t like humans at all but can only see them if they are directly in front of him because he has really poor vision from being in the dark all the time. However, when he does see humans, he will swim the other way. Maki also will not eat the food humans feed him even though it looks exactly like the food particles because it smells slightly different from the actual ones. He might have poor visions but his sense of smell is very good!
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature It’s for the absolute best if you don’t interact with Maki. He doesn’t like humans at all and will swim in the opposite direction when he sees humans. Like any other Makiatos, his fear for humans started when he saw his companions get killed by humans for their wings right in front of him. Over the years, they’ve gotten more and more isolated and afraid of other beings.
